Sustainable Transportation

From Hunters Point to the Sunset, from SOMA to Twin Peaks, a good portion of San Francisco takes the bus at least twice a week. However, not enough people take the bus to negate the negative effects of cars. People going across the bay use fuel-hungry cars and are a bigger chunk of SF's carbon footprint. Learn about how you can help to reduce this.
